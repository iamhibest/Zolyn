How Zolyn works

01

Every school's data stays private. No school can ever see another school's students, results, or records.

02

Joining a school needs approval. A School Admin reviews and approves every teacher request before access is granted.

03

Report cards are reviewed before download. You'll always see the finished report on screen first, and only download it when you're satisfied.
